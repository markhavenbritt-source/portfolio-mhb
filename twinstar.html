Twin Star Branding

PROBLEM: A new digital-first comics platform needed a complete brand identity—one that matched its modern ambition and challenged the legacy aesthetics of the comics industry.

SOLUTION: Designed a full brand system built around four core traits: Confident, Unpretentious, Sharp, and Disruptive—expressed through a bold angular logo, high-contrast palette, and clean contemporary typography.

ROLE: Creative Director

How We Did It

Defined four brand pillars

Worked with the founding team to distill TWIN STAR's character into four defining traits: Confident, Unpretentious, Sharp, and Disruptive. These became the lens through which every visual decision was filtered.

Designed a logo built for motion

Created a bold, angular monogram engineered to work across static and animated contexts. The mark is lean, modern, and signals a brand built to move fast.

Built the full visual system

Developed palette, typography, and application standards to create a cohesive identity that scales. Clean, contemporary, and built for creators and fans who expect more than nostalgia loops and dated workflows.

Impact

Delivered a complete brand system from concept to finished identity

Visual language positions TWIN STAR as a modern alternative to legacy comics platforms

Early mark explorations document the full evolution of the logo, from sketch to final form
