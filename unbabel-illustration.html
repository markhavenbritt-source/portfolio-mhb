Unbabel Blog Illustrations

PROBLEM: Unbabel's marketing blog needed a steady stream of custom illustrations to elevate the content—week after week, without missing deadlines.

SOLUTION: Established a visual system and creative brainstorming process that enabled illustrators to develop ideas quickly, explore diverse rendering styles, and consistently deliver on time.

ROLE: Creative Director

How We Did It

Set the visual direction for the blog

Defined an overall illustration style that gave the blog a coherent look while allowing individual pieces to breathe. The system made it easier to brief and faster to execute each week.

Ran structured brainstorming sessions

Brought writers and illustrators together to formulate concepts collaboratively—ensuring ideas were strong before execution started, and reducing back-and-forth during production.

Explored diverse rendering approaches

Encouraged hand-drawn, collage, and other rendering solutions to keep the work from going stale. Variety was part of the brief—and helped develop each illustrator's range over time.

Impact

Produced a consistent flow of custom illustrations for Unbabel's marketing blog

Met every deadline through a repeatable, team-driven creative process

Developed illustrators' range by encouraging and evaluating multiple rendering styles

PARTNERS: Megan Yama (Illustrator), Bruno Silva (Illustrator)
