Unbabel eBooks

PROBLEM: Unbabel's gated eBook content was a key lead-generation tool, but the design needed to look polished enough to be worth downloading—and credible enough to convert.

SOLUTION: Gave senior designers creative ownership over individual eBook designs, providing direction, coaching through the process, and ensuring high-quality output that met both marketing and brand standards.

ROLE: Creative Director

How We Did It

Delegated with intention

Used each eBook as a development opportunity. Gave senior designers real creative ownership with broad guidelines, then supported their ideation and execution process throughout.

Coached through the process

Provided feedback at key stages and helped designers navigate stakeholder collaboration—building skills that extended well beyond the immediate project and into day-to-day creative practice.

Maintained quality and consistency

Ensured each piece met Unbabel's visual standards while allowing creative variation across titles. Consistent enough to build trust, flexible enough to stay interesting.

Impact

Produced a library of high-quality gated eBooks that drove consistent lead generation

Used the work as a vehicle to develop junior designers into senior-level collaborators

Strengthened the team's ability to work independently and with stakeholders

PARTNERS: Brent Hurlbert (Senior Designer), Tom Groom (Senior Designer)
