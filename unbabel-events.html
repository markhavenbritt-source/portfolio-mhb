Unbabel Events

PROBLEM: The previous year's LangOps Universe conference branding lacked punch and didn't reflect the scale or energy the event deserved.

SOLUTION: Led the team through a collaborative creative process, pitching multiple bold directions and fostering a culture of shared ownership. Also developed a scaleable road show booth package for Unbabel's broader event circuit.

ROLE: Creative Director

How We Did It

Ran a full-team creative pitch

Brought the whole team into the creative process: sharing trends, discussing inspiration, and pitching multiple directions. This wasn't just about output—it was about building design culture.

Balanced direction with development

Was hands-on when refinement mattered, and stepped back to let the team lead. The goal was a more collaborative, higher-functioning creative team alongside a stronger event brand.

Directed the Road Show booth package

Managed a Senior Designer through a parallel project: a scaleable booth system adjustable to different audiences and venues across Unbabel's event circuit.

Impact

Elevated branding for Unbabel's signature LangOps Universe conference

Delivered a scaleable, multi-venue road show event booth system

Built a stronger collaborative design culture through shared creative process

PARTNERS: Bruno Silva (Art Director), Brent Hurlbert (Senior Designer), Tom Groom (Senior Designer), Griff McLaughlin (Intern), Max Kaufman (Project Manager), Phil Hunter (Copywriter), Arlan Smith (Motion Designer)
