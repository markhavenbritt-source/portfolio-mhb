Possibility Lab Logo

PROBLEM: The Possibility Lab, a University of California research group focused on public policy, needed a new brand identity that matched the innovation and rigor of its work.

SOLUTION: Developed a logo, palette, and typeface system built around three core values—creative problem-solving, data-driven public good, and optimism about the future.

ROLE: Creative Director

How We Did It

Distilled the brand to three core values

Worked with the Possibility Lab to clarify what they stood for: creative problem-solving, data-driven impact, and a bright future for all. These became the foundation for every design decision.

Designed a custom mark

Created a bespoke "P" monogram using overlapping circles to represent both data and the public. The form is optimistic, open, and intellectually precise—right for an institution that informs policy.

Aligned palette and type to the mission

Selected type and color that communicate innovation and credibility. The full system works for an institution that partners with universities and publishes rigorous public-good research.

Impact

Delivered a complete brand identity system for a leading UC research group

Logo clearly communicates the lab's public-good mission and data-driven approach

Early iterations included to show the full evolution of the mark from concept to final
